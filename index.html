What It Does

One platform that adapts to whatever your farm needs

Describe It, It Gets Built

Need a fertilizer calculator? A frost alert system? An equipment monitor? Just describe what you want in plain English. FFT Nano writes the code, deploys it, and runs it. In minutes, not months.

Works With Every Piece of Equipment You Own

That 1998 grain elevator talks to your 2024 fleet system. Old equipment, new sensors, cloud services — FFT Nano bridges them all automatically. No rip-and-replace required.

Gets Smarter Every Season

FFT Nano learns from your soil, your crops, your equipment, and your decisions. It optimizes workflows over time, spots patterns you'd miss, and remembers everything so you don't have to.

Why FFT Nano

Built for farmers who want control, not complexity

Your Farm, Your Data

FFT Nano runs on your hardware. Your data never leaves your property. No cloud dependency, no subscription fees, no vendor lock-in. You own everything — the software, the data, and the decisions.

Open Source & Free

MIT licensed. Free to use, modify, and share. No usage limits, no feature gates, no surprise bills. If you can run a Raspberry Pi, you can run FFT Nano.

You Stay in the Driver's Seat

FFT Nano automates the repetitive work — checking sensors, logging data, coordinating equipment. You make the strategic decisions. It's your tool, not your replacement.
